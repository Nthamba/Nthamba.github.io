About Me

Eng. Susan Masila is a highly experienced WASH Specialist with a strong background in environmental engineering and development program management. My career spans over a decade, focusing on sustainable water resource management, sanitation, and hygiene initiatives across various challenging environments in Africa.

My expertise includes leading USAID's efforts to increase access to WASH services in Kenya, providing technical and strategic leadership from policy analysis to program design. I am adept at integrating environmental management practices into engineering solutions for water and wastewater systems, with a keen focus on nature-based solutions.

I am passionate about driving measurable impact, fostering learning, and building capacity within teams and communities to achieve sustainable development goals. My structured thinking, intellectual curiosity, and team-oriented mindset enable me to translate complex strategies into actionable insights and measurable results.
